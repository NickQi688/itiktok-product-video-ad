# CoreGLP German Copy Patterns

Load this reference for CoreGLP scripts, hooks, voiceover, subtitles, captions, publishing copy, and German TikTok variants.

## Extraction Rule

Borrow the conversion mechanism from competitor copy, not its factual claims or exact wording. Build energetic, emotionally specific copy while keeping CoreGLP framed as part of a routine rather than a guaranteed weight-loss treatment.

## High-Conversion Mechanisms

### Seasonal reset

Use spring, summer, holiday, event, or wardrobe-change timing as an emotional deadline.

Useful angles:
- `Dieser Sommer fühlt sich anders an.`
- `Dein Sommer-Outfit wartet nicht auf Montag.`
- `Neue Saison. Neue Routine. Weniger Drama.`

Do not default to a calendar visual. Express the season through sunlight, wardrobe changes, travel packing, fitting rooms, invitations, or outdoor plans.

### Wardrobe future pacing

Let the viewer imagine reclaiming an outfit or feeling more comfortable before introducing the product.

Structure:
1. Open the wardrobe or fitting-room curtain.
2. Reveal the ignored dress, jeans, or event outfit.
3. Show the emotional hesitation.
4. Introduce a simple daily routine with CoreGLP.
5. End on confidence, preparation, or action instead of an instant body transformation.

Safe German cues:
- `Kennst du dieses Kleid ganz hinten im Schrank?`
- `Nicht das Kleid ist das Problem. Das Aufschieben ist es.`
- `Mein Ziel hängt schon da. Jetzt bekommt es einen Plan.`

### Jo-Jo frustration reframe

Start from the repeated stop-start experience without diagnosing metabolism or promising permanent results.

Structure:
1. `Schon wieder von vorne?`
2. Reject crash-diet drama.
3. Reframe toward a repeatable routine.
4. Place CoreGLP inside that routine.
5. End with a small next step.

Safe German cues:
- `Nicht noch eine Alles-oder-nichts-Woche.`
- `Ich brauchte keinen neuen Montag. Ich brauchte eine Routine.`
- `Routine statt Jo-Jo-Drama.`

### Future-self visualization

Use concrete sensory details: opening the wardrobe, fastening jeans, packing for a trip, walking into sunlight, leaving home without changing outfits three times.

Keep the payoff emotional and behavioral:
- calmer choice
- consistent routine
- confidence
- less indecision
- feeling prepared

Do not show a capsule causing an immediate slimmer body.

### Social proof without fabricated numbers

Use social proof only when verified source material exists. Without proof, use relatable community language instead of invented counts.

Allowed without statistics:
- `Viele kennen genau diesen Neustart-Moment.`
- `Diese Routine taucht gerade überall in meinem Feed auf.`
- `Ich wollte wissen, warum so viele darüber sprechen.`

Require approved evidence before using exact participant counts, customer counts, clinical-study language, ratings, or order volume.

### Simple daily action

Show one capsule, water, bottle placement, or a prepared routine as visible behavior. Avoid implying that one capsule replaces food choices, exercise, sleep, or medical care.

Useful cues:
- `Ein kleiner Schritt in meiner täglichen Routine.`
- `CoreGLP, Wasser, weiter geht's.`
- `Kein Zaubertrick. Ein Plan, den ich wiederholen kann.`

### Anti-quick-fix and step-by-step transformation

Reject the exhausting cycle of dramatic restarts, then make gradual progress feel decisive rather than timid.

Useful angles:
- `Keine neue Crash-Diät. Ein Schritt, den ich morgen wiederholen kann.`
- `Nicht perfekt. Aber endlich konsequent.`
- `Schritt fur Schritt statt alles oder nichts.`
- `Fortschritt schlagt den nachsten Neustart.`

The visual payoff should be a completed behavior, outfit decision, prepared routine, or confident action. Do not depict the capsule instantly reshaping a body.

### Plant-based routine positioning

Use `pflanzlich` or `mit pflanzlichen Inhaltsstoffen` only when confirmed by the product label or approved product data. Make the formula a reason to investigate or a routine cue, not proof of weight-loss efficacy or safety.

Useful cues when substantiated:
- `Pflanzliche Inhaltsstoffe. Ein klarer Platz in meiner Routine.`
- `Meine Routine beginnt mit einem kleinen Schritt.`

Do not equate herbal or plant-based with harmless, medically effective, liver-healing, detoxifying, or suitable for everyone.

### Inside-out metaphor

`Von innen heraus` can be used as an emotional metaphor for routine, energy, self-care, or confidence. It must not silently become an organ, detox, skin-treatment, or disease claim.

Safer cues:
- `Veränderung beginnt nicht im Spiegel.`
- `Erst die Routine. Dann das neue Gefuhl.`
- `Von innen anfangen. Im Alltag dranbleiben.`

### Cost and effort contrast

Contrast the simplicity of a daily routine with the mental cost of repeated restarts. Price comparisons must use current verified prices and comparable periods.

Useful angles:
- `Was kostet dich der nachste Neustart?`
- `Einfacher als mein altes Alles-oder-nichts.`
- `Weniger Drama. Mehr Routine.`

Do not claim `günstiger als ein Monat Fitnessstudio` unless both prices and the comparison basis are verified for the target market.

### Strong CTA rhythm

Use urgency around the viewer's decision or a verified offer, not fabricated demand.

Safer CTA patterns:
- `Nicht nächsten Montag. Heute anfangen.`
- `Dein Ziel-Outfit wartet. CoreGLP ansehen.`
- `Mach aus dem Wunsch einen Plan.`
- `Mehr erfahren.`
- `Angebot ansehen.` only when an actual offer exists.

## 15-Second Structures

### A. Wardrobe micro-drama

- 0-2s: ignored dress or jeans hits the floor; `Schon wieder versteckt?`
- 2-5s: mirror hesitation or outfit pile
- 5-9s: CoreGLP bottle, water, one clear routine action
- 9-12s: goal outfit placed visibly for tomorrow
- 12-15s: `Routine statt Drama. CoreGLP ansehen.`

### B. Jo-Jo comment reply

- 0-2s: comment overlay; `Du fängst sowieso wieder von vorne an.`
- 2-4s: direct reply; `Nein. Diesmal nicht alles auf einmal.`
- 4-9s: three quick routine beats: water, CoreGLP, snack/meal plan or walk
- 9-12s: confident exit or prepared outfit
- 12-15s: `Kein Crash. Ein Plan.`

### C. Seasonal invitation countdown

- 0-2s: invitation, suitcase, beach bag, or summer dress; no weight number
- 2-5s: `Dieser Sommer beginnt nicht am Strand. Er beginnt mit meiner Routine.`
- 5-10s: product and daily-action montage
- 10-13s: sunlight/outdoor confidence payoff
- 13-15s: `Heute anfangen. Mehr erfahren.`

### D. Anti-ad confession

- 0-3s: `Ich wollte keine weitere Abnehm-Werbung sehen.`
- 3-6s: creator closes exaggerated ad or pushes away crash-diet props
- 6-10s: `Also habe ich mir eine Routine gebaut, die in meinen Alltag passt.`
- 10-13s: CoreGLP product action
- 13-15s: `Plan statt Versprechen.`

### E. Jo-Jo boxing match

- 0-2s: creator enters a mock boxing ring against a giant `JO-JO` prop; `Runde 12? Nicht mit mir.`
- 2-5s: fast flashbacks of crash-diet props flying into the corner
- 5-9s: bell rings; water, CoreGLP, and one repeatable routine action replace the props
- 9-12s: creator steps out of the ring instead of fighting another round
- 12-15s: `Raus aus dem Neustart. Rein in die Routine.`

### F. Inside-out control-room metaphor

- 0-2s: animated body-shaped control room flashes `ALLES ODER NICHTS`
- 2-5s: levers spin chaotically; creator hits stop
- 5-9s: one daily routine sequence appears as three stable illuminated controls
- 9-12s: the control room settles; no medical organ visualization
- 12-15s: `Nicht perfekt. Aber endlich konsequent.`

## Unsafe Source Claim Rewrites

| Do not reuse | Rewrite direction |
| --- | --- |
| `14 kg in 3 Wochen garantiert` | Remove the number, timeframe, and guarantee; focus on starting a routine now. |
| `klinisch bestätigt mit 27.000 Teilnehmern` | Use only with approved evidence; otherwise remove entirely. |
| `5x schnellere Fettverbrennung` | Avoid multiplier and fat-burning causality; use routine or metabolic-balance support language only if approved. |
| `sichtbare Ergebnisse nach wenigen Tagen` | Replace with consistency, preparation, or confidence; do not promise visible change. |
| `entgiftet den Körper` | Remove detox claims. |
| `kein Fitnessstudio, keine Diät, kein Hunger` | Say the routine is simple or fits daily life; do not imply effortless guaranteed loss. |
| `reduziert Heißhunger` | Use only when specifically approved; otherwise show snack-conflict as a narrative metaphor without claiming appetite suppression. |
| `tausende haben heute bestellt` | Use only with verified live sales data; otherwise remove. |
| `das letzte Mal abnehmen` | Replace with `ein Plan, den ich wiederholen kann`; do not guarantee permanence. |
| `shed kilos in week 1` | Remove the result and timeframe unless the exact outcome is substantiated for the advertised population and use conditions. |
| `lasting results / no rebound` | Reframe as consistency or weight-management support; never guarantee permanent maintenance. |
| `heal your liver` | Do not use without authorized medical evidence and market approval; default to removing the organ-healing claim entirely. |
| `skin glow from the inside out` | Use only as subjective lifestyle imagery, not as a physiological product effect. |
| `herbal / plant-based formula` | Use only when the ingredient record supports it; do not treat it as efficacy or safety evidence. |
| `cheaper than a month at the gym` | Use only with dated, market-specific, like-for-like price evidence. |

## Evidence-Locked Hard Claims

Hard promises may be used only when the evidence pack is complete. A competitor's wording is inspiration, never substantiation.

Required evidence-pack fields:
- exact claim wording approved for publication
- evidence ID and accessible source document
- study design and named responsible organization
- participant population and sample size
- product/formula identity matching the advertised product
- dosage and usage conditions
- study duration and outcome definition
- absolute result plus relevant baseline, not only a relative multiplier
- target country/market and applicable disclaimers
- written legal/platform approval and approval date

Claims requiring this gate include:
- exact kg/lbs in an exact number of days or weeks
- `garantiert`, `zweifelsfrei bestätigt`, or equivalent certainty
- clinical-study participant counts
- `kein Jo-Jo-Effekt`, `kein Rebound`, or permanent maintenance
- liver healing, detoxification, disease, hormone, or organ claims
- guaranteed appetite suppression or fat-burning multipliers

When the pack is incomplete, output both:
1. `高风险参考，不可直接发布: <source claim>`
2. a high-energy non-quantified rewrite, for example:
   - `3 Wochen. Eine Routine. Wie weit gehst du diesmal?`
   - `Sag dem Jo-Jo-Neustart den Kampf an.`
   - `Dein erster Unterschied beginnt heute, nicht irgendwann.`
   - `Keine Wunderzahl. Eine Routine mit Richtung.`

## Language Integrity

- Write normal German characters.
- Do not substitute Cyrillic or lookalike Unicode characters to evade moderation.
- Avoid copied paragraph cadence across variants.
- Keep spoken lines short enough for the assigned shot duration.
- Prefer 4-9 words per on-screen line.
- Use one emotional promise per clip: confidence, consistency, wardrobe readiness, seasonal reset, or less routine chaos.

## Batch Diversity Check

Before finalizing multiple CoreGLP scripts, ensure the batch includes different structures. Across five scripts, use at least four of these:
- short drama
- comment reply
- anti-ad confession
- anime or metaphor
- product dossier
- fake live stream
- seasonal invitation
- wardrobe/fitting-room conflict
- snack-conflict comedy
- UGC rant

Do not repeat the same hook prop, CTA sentence, or emotional payoff in more than two scripts.
